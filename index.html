    About Me




    Originally I am from Zimbabwe but I am currently based in Cape Town, South Africa. I started my journey as a fine art atrist six years ago, after having spent time pondering one of the biggest question anyone can ask themself, "What am I really good at or what is my gift/talent?" I had spent years asking myself this question but having prayed, yes, I prayed for God to give me a gift/talent, something that I can say I enjoy doing and I'm good at. And I guess His finger landed on fine art!

    I specialize in the use of pencil and charcol on my drawings, which sort of differentiates me from other artist but most importantly it helps to showcase that realism effect on my drawing.
